Our Story

Where ancient wisdom meets modern life

Founded in 2012, Praana Yoga grew from a small rooftop practice into a thriving community of over 800 members. We believe yoga is not a performance — it is a daily conversation between you and your body.

Our certified instructors draw from Hatha, Ashtanga, Kundalini, and Yin traditions. Whether you're stepping onto the mat for the first time or deepening a lifelong practice, there is a class shaped for you.
